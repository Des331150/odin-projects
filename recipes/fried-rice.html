Description

Fried rice is a very popular dish across the world and is enjoyed by millions around the world.

It is easy to make and is a budget option for people who are on a tight budget.

Ingredients

  • Day old rice
  • Eggs
  • Vegetable oil
  • Green onions
  • Carrots
  • Peas
  • Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Get a wok or a frying pan and place it on the fire.
  2. Add two and a half cups of vegetable oil to the wok or pan.
  3. Once it is hot, add your:
    • Onions(chopped)
    • Green onions(chopped)
    • Carrots(diced or grated depending on what you want)
    • Peas
  4. Saute them about three mins and remove them from the fire.
  5. Crack your eggs into the pan and scramble them for about a minute.
  6. Next, add your rice and beat it in order to loosen the rice grains.
  7. Stir fry the rice and eggs for about two mins and add two and a half spoons of your soy sauce into the mix
  8. Add your vegetables and cook for about three mins and occasionally tossing the pan or wok.
  9. Get a plate and pour your delicious fried rice on it.
  10. Optionally, add some spring onions and voila, your food is ready.
