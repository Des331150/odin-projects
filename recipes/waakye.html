Description of waakye

Waakye is a local dish prepared in Ghana.It is made up of rice and beans and is eaten with stew and pepper.

It is usually garnished with egg,salad,spaghetti and gari.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Beans
  • Egg
  • Spaghetti
  • Gari

Steps

  1. Wash your beans and place it on the fire.
  2. Just when the beans is about to turn soft, add your rice(wash your rice) to the beans and add a bit of water
  3. Turn the heat to low and let it cook for about 30 mins.
  4. Whiles the rice is cooking, put a pot filled with water on the stove.
  5. Once it starts to boil, add your spaghetti to the boiling water.
  6. Season it with salt and let it cook for 10 mins.
  7. After 10 mins, take the pot off from the fire and drain the water.
  8. After 30 mins, the waakye should be cooked through.
  9. Place your egg in some water and let it boil for 10 mins
  10. Fetch a couple of spoons of your waakye, add your egg,spaghetti and gari. Add stew and pepper and some salad(optional)
  11. Voila, you have a delicious plate of waakye in front of you.
