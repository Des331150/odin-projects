Description

Porridge is a popular food in Ghana. It is made from either corn dough,millet or wheat.

For this recipe, we will be using corn dough to prepare this porridge.

Ingredients

For this recipe, the ingredients that will be used are:
  • Corn dough
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Get a large pan and pour your corn dough in it. Add water and mix by hand to ensure the dough completely dissolves in the water.
  2. After mixing it, place it on a stove on high heat.
  3. Get a ladle and stir your mixture till the dough begins to thicken.
  4. Add some water occasionally and continue to stir the thick mixture until it becomes smooth with the right consistency
  5. When that is achieved, reduce the heat to low and leave it to simmer for about 5 mins.
  6. After that, it is ready to be served.
  7. Fetch a couple spoons of your porridge into a bowl and add some sugar and milk to your liking.
  8. You can also add groundnuts(optional) and voila, your delicious porridge is ready to be consumed.
